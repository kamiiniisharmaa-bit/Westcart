🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters at Westcart. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Westcart ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with our services. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using Westcart, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use our Site or services. We may update this policy from time to time; any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide, improve, and personalize your experience at Westcart:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, and account credentials.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, digital wallet info (processed securely via third-party PCI-compliant gateways; we do not store full card numbers).
  • Order History: Products purchased, returns, preferences, and wishlist items.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and cookies (see Section 5).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, search queries, and referral sources.
  • Communications: Customer support interactions, surveys, and feedback submissions.
Our promise: We never sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

Westcart uses collected data for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders, returns, and exchanges.
  • Sending order confirmations, shipping updates, and important service messages.
  • Improving our website, products, and customer service.
  • Personalizing your shopping experience (recommendations, saved carts).
  • Preventing fraud, unauthorized transactions, and ensuring security.
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms & Conditions.
  • With your consent: sending promotional emails, newsletters, and exclusive offers (you can opt out anytime).

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that help operate our business (e.g., payment processors, shipping carriers, IT hosting, email delivery). These parties are contractually bound to safeguard your data.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction (with notice).
  • With Your Consent: Any other case where you explicitly authorize us to share your information.

We do not rent or sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience

Westcart uses cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyze trends, and improve site performance. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings; however, disabling essential cookies may affect certain features (e.g., shopping cart).

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality (login, checkout).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and region.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to show relevant product recommendations (only with consent).

By using our Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described. You can withdraw consent by clearing cookies or adjusting browser settings.

6. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR for EU residents, CCPA for California), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal exceptions (e.g., fraud prevention).
  • Opt-out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your data, but you can opt out of targeted advertising.
  • Restriction & Objection: Limit how we use your data in certain situations.
  • Withdraw Consent: For marketing emails, you can unsubscribe via the link in any email or contact us.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Team at privacy@Westcart.com or use the form below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security & Retention

Westcart takes data security seriously. We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — including SSL encryption, firewalls, and access controls — to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. For example, order information is retained for tax, accounting, and warranty purposes. After this period, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your account credentials confidential and notify us immediately of any suspected breach.

8. Children's Privacy

Westcart does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has submitted information should contact us at privacy@Westcart.com.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., social media platforms, payment partners). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email (if you have an account) or through a notice on our homepage. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when revisions were made. Your continued use of Westcart after changes constitute acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to our Privacy Team:

Email: privacy@Westcart.com
Privacy Hotline: +1 (800) 555-PRIV
Data Protection Officer: 123 Commerce Ave, Suite 450, Wilmington, DE 19801, USA
